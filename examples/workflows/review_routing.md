We keep having the problem where a PR sits for two days because it went to the wrong person or nobody picked it up. What I want is: when a PR is opened, look at what files changed and figure out who actually owns that part of the codebase (frontend stuff goes to the frontend folks, infra/deploy stuff goes to whoever handles that, etc), and assign it to them or at least ping them directly instead of just relying on the generic review request. If it touches something like auth or payments that's sensitive, flag it as needing a second reviewer too, not just one person rubber-stamping it. Small stuff like docs or a one-line config change can probably just go to whoever's around. I don't need it to actually merge anything or approve anything, just get it in front of the right eyeballs fast.
