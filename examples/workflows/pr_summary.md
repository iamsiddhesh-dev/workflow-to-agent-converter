Our PRs are getting big enough that reviewers don't actually read the whole diff, they just skim and approve, and stuff slips through. I want a summary generated for every PR that actually explains what changed and why it matters - not just "updated files X, Y, Z" but like, what's the actual behavior change, does it touch anything risky (migrations, auth, anything that hits prod config), and are there tests covering the change or not. Post it as a comment on the PR so reviewers have something real to start from instead of the raw diff. If the diff is small and obviously trivial (typo fix, comment change) it doesn't need the full treatment, just a one-liner is fine.
