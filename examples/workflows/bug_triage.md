Bugs come in from a bunch of places - support tickets, Sentry alerts, sometimes just someone on the team noticing something's off - and right now whoever notices first just kind of shouts about it in Slack and it's inconsistent whether it gets tracked properly. I want incoming bug reports looked at and triaged: figure out roughly how severe it is (is prod down for everyone, or is this an edge case one customer hit), guess which part of the system it's probably in based on the description or stack trace, and file it with a severity label and a suggested owner. If it looks like a P0 - actual outage, data loss, security-shaped - that needs a human to confirm before it goes anywhere, I don't want the system unilaterally paging someone at 2am on a bad guess. Everything else can just get filed and the team picks it up in normal planning.
